Best Transport System

Electronic Transport System is a software and mobile app designed for transport companies; to enable them to electronically manage their transport activities and for their customers to have the ease of real time access to the transportation company so as to book and manage tickets for a journey and to hire vehicles to the destination of their choice in their comfort via technological devices (PCs/Macs, tablets, and phones).
